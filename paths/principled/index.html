Why First Principles?

Most people learn Bitcoin backwards — starting with wallets, transactions, and technical details. The Principled Path takes you deeper: we start with the laws of physics, human nature, and information theory.

By the end, you won't just understand how Bitcoin works — you'll understand why it must exist.

⚡
Energy
Value requires work. Digital scarcity must be anchored in physical reality.
🎯
Incentives
Systems fail when incentives reward betrayal. Bitcoin aligns self-interest with honesty.
📡
Information
Truth requires verification. Centralized trust doesn't scale.
🌀
Emergence
Bitcoin isn't designed — it evolves. Complex order from simple rules.
