‚úçÔ∏è How to Write Your Backup (Do This Now)

Materials Needed:

  • 2-3 pieces of quality paper (acid-free if possible)
  • Pen (NOT pencil - can smudge/fade)
  • Quiet, private space (no cameras, no people)

Writing Process:

  1. Open your wallet app and view your seed phrase
  2. Write WORD 1 carefully, exactly as shown
  3. Continue through all words (12 or 24)
  4. Number each word (1, 2, 3... order matters!)
  5. Write clearly - if you can't read it later, it's useless
  6. Make 2-3 identical copies

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Mixing up word order (word 7 in position 12 = wrong wallet)
  • Misspelling words ("their" vs "there" - wrong word!)
  • Using bad handwriting (you won't be able to read it in 5 years)
  • Storing all copies in the same location

üß™ Test Your Backup (CRITICAL)

DO NOT SKIP THIS STEP. Many people lose bitcoin because they never tested their backup and made a mistake when writing it down.

Recovery Test Process:

  1. Delete your wallet from the app (don't worry - you'll recover it)
  2. Re-open the wallet app
  3. Choose "Recover from seed phrase" or "Import wallet"
  4. Enter your 12 or 24 words from your paper backup
  5. Confirm your bitcoin balance appears correctly

Why This Test Matters

If you made ANY mistake writing down your seed phrase, you'll discover it now - while you still have access to your funds. If you wait years and then discover the backup doesn't work, your bitcoin is gone forever.

This 5-minute test could save you thousands of dollars.
