Immediate Custody

Time Required: 8 minutes

Why This Matters

  • Bitcoin on an exchange is NOT truly yours
  • The exchange can freeze, lose, or get hacked
  • "Get your Bitcoin off exchanges" - this is THE rule
  • Moving to self-custody takes 8 minutes and protects you forever

‚öôÔ∏è Setup Your Wallet (3 minutes)

Step 1: Download & Install

Go to your phone's app store. Search for "Blue Wallet" (or Muun/Phoenix). Download and open the app.

Step 2: Create New Wallet

Tap "Add Wallet" or "Create Wallet". Choose "Bitcoin" (not Lightning yet). Give it a name like "My Bitcoin".

Step 3: Write Down Your Seed Phrase (CRITICAL)

The app will show you 12 or 24 words. This is your seed phrase or recovery phrase.

‚ö†Ô∏è CRITICAL: Seed Phrase Rules

  • Write it on PAPER (not phone, not computer, not cloud)
  • Write it EXACTLY as shown (order matters, spelling matters)
  • Store it somewhere SAFE (we'll improve storage in Module 3)
  • NEVER type it into any website or app except your wallet
  • NEVER share it with anyone - not even "support"

Step 4: Verify Your Backup

The wallet will ask you to confirm your seed phrase. This ensures you wrote it correctly. Don't skip this step!

üì¨ Get Your First Bitcoin Address (1 minute)

A Bitcoin address is like an email address for receiving bitcoin.

  1. Open your new wallet
  2. Tap "Receive" or the receive icon
  3. You'll see a QR code and a long string starting with "bc1" or "3"
  4. This is your address! You can receive bitcoin to this address.

üß™ Try It: Check Bitcoin Address Formats

See what different Bitcoin address types look like:

Click a button to generate a sample address

What to look for: Your wallet will likely show a bc1 address (best) or 3 address (also good). Avoid wallets that only generate "1" addresses - they're outdated.

What's This Address?

  • It's like a bank account number, but better
  • You can share it publicly - it's safe
  • Each wallet can generate unlimited addresses
  • All addresses connect to the same wallet

Withdraw From Exchange to Your Wallet (3 minutes)

Now move your bitcoin from Cash App (or Coinbase) to YOUR wallet.

From Cash App:

  1. Open Cash App ‚Üí Bitcoin section
  2. Tap your balance ‚Üí "Withdraw Bitcoin"
  3. Paste your wallet address (or scan QR code)
  4. Enter the amount (start with your test $20)
  5. Confirm and wait 10-30 minutes for blockchain confirmation

From Coinbase:

  1. Open Coinbase ‚Üí Bitcoin balance
  2. Tap "Send" ‚Üí "Send Bitcoin"
  3. Paste your wallet address
  4. Enter amount ‚Üí Review ‚Üí Confirm
  5. Wait for confirmation (10-30 minutes)

‚ö†Ô∏è Double-Check Address!

  • Bitcoin transactions are IRREVERSIBLE
  • Always verify the first and last 4 characters of the address
  • Use copy/paste or QR code - NEVER type manually
  • Start with small amounts until you're confident
