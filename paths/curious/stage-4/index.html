Imagine this scenario:

Someone discovers you own Bitcoin. Maybe you bragged on social media, or your name appeared in an exchange leak. One day, they show up with a wrench and a simple demand:

"Transfer your Bitcoin to me, or else."

This isn't science fiction. This is the reality of being your own bank. No Bitcoin security is perfect, but in this stage, you'll learn:

  • How to protect your keys from digital attacks
  • How to protect yourself from physical attacks
  • How to recover if disaster strikes
  • How to plan for the unthinkable

Security isn't optional. It's survival.

Why Security Matters More Than Ever

With Bitcoin, you are the bank. There's no customer service to call if you get hacked. No insurance policy to cover stolen funds. No "forgot password" button.

The good news? Bitcoin's security model is incredibly robust IF you follow best practices. Most Bitcoin losses come from user error, not protocol failures.

In this final stage of The Curious Path, you'll master the fundamentals of Bitcoin security so you can confidently hold and use Bitcoin for years to come.
