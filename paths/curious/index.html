Your Bitcoin Learning Journey

Welcome to The Curious Path! This comprehensive program takes you from zero knowledge to confident Bitcoin ownership in 8 weeks. Learn at your own pace through 4 stages and 13 interactive modules.

Each stage builds on the previous one, teaching you not just what Bitcoin is, but how it works, how to use it, and how to keep it safe.

🌱

Stage 1: Understanding Money

⏱️ 1-2 weeks 📚 4 modules 🎓 ~85 minutes
Start Here

Before diving into Bitcoin, let's build a foundation. Understanding what money is, why it matters, and what makes good money will make Bitcoin's innovation crystal clear.

📖 What You'll Learn:

Module 1: What is Money? (History, properties, importance)
Module 2: Problems with Traditional Money (Inflation, centralization, control)
Module 3: Enter Bitcoin (Origin story, how it solves problems)
Module 4: Building a Bank Without Bankers (Reinvent Bitcoin from scratch using analogies)
