Frequently asked

Will this teach me how to set up a hardware wallet?

The kit assumes you can follow your wallet manufacturer's setup. What it adds is the verification, backup-test, and recovery layer that no manufacturer ships in the box.

Multisig, single-sig, or both?

Both, with a worksheet to help you decide which is right for you given your sats size, threat model, and how much your future self wants to maintain.

The checklists are wallet-agnostic. The Sparrow/Electrum-specific notes appear where relevant.
