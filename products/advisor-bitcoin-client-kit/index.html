Frequently asked

I have one Bitcoin client. Is this overkill?

Even one self-custodying client requires the same documented process. The intake, classifier, and scorecard are the same whether you have one or thirty. Most advisors start with one and acquire more once their existing client tells someone they have an advisor who actually understands the custody side.

Will this teach me how to set up multisig?

No. The kit is for advisors who do NOT need to set up custody — and should not be the ones setting it up. It is the layer above: how you talk to a client about their custody, score it, and document it.

What about the ETF question?

Covered explicitly. Includes the conversation script for "I'll just buy the ETF" versus self-custody, with implications across the eight dimensions advisors care about.

Is this regulated advice?

Process and conversation infrastructure. Not legal, tax, or investment advice. Your compliance officer should review the engagement-letter clauses before deployment.

Can I customize the templates with my firm logo?

The .docx templates are designed for that. Single-advisor license covers internal and client-facing use. Resale or repackaging is not licensed at this tier — see BSA for Advisors for white-label.
