Frequently Asked Questions

Can we try before we buy?

Yes! All our demos are available for free on the public site. You can evaluate them before licensing. We also offer 30-day pilot programs for organizations.

What's the difference between co-branded and white-label?

Co-branded shows both your logo and BSA's. White-label removes all BSA branding‚Äîit appears as your own tool. White-label is available on Professional and Enterprise tiers.

Can we modify the content?

Teaching guides can be adapted freely. Demo content is locked to ensure educational accuracy, but Enterprise customers can request custom modifications.

How do users access the demos?

We provide a private URL or embed codes. For white-label, we can deploy to your subdomain. No user accounts required unless you want progress tracking.

Is there a non-profit discount?

Yes, registered non-profits and educational institutions receive 25% off all tiers. Contact us with proof of status.

What about translations?

Most demos are available in English. We're working on Spanish and Portuguese. Custom translations can be arranged for Enterprise customers.
