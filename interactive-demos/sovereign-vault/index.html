What is Sovereign Vault?

Sovereign Vault is NOT a wallet. It does not store your Bitcoin, seed phrases, or private keys.

It is a custody intelligence system that helps you:

  • Document where your backups are stored (using aliases)
  • Track your wallet configurations
  • Plan for inheritance and emergencies
  • Monitor your overall custody resilience

Think of it as a secure, encrypted "map" of your custody setup‚Äînot the treasure itself.
