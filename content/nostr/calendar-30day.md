**Week 1 thread (post on D04 Thu, 14:00 UTC):**

> The 6 questions every Bitcoin custody plan has to answer
> 1/ Who signs?
> 2/ Where do the backups live, physically?
> 3/ Who knows what — and who is allowed to know later?
> 4/ What happens if a device fails?
> 5/ What happens if a person fails (illness, coercion, panic)?
> 6/ What happens when you're not the one answering?
> Most plans answer 1 and 2. The remaining 4 are where families lose Bitcoin.
> Free walkthrough: https://bitcoinsovereign.academy/paths/sovereign/

body: |
  Inflation isn't the number on the news.

  That's the average. Your inflation is the basket you actually buy.

  Rent, food, schooling, healthcare, energy &mdash; weight them by how much of your income each one eats, and you get a number that doesn't appear in any headline.

  Most people are losing more purchasing power than the report says, and earning less than the wage data implies. The unit lies in both directions.
