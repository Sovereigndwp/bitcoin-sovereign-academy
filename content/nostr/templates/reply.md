# Template — Reply note

**Goal:** add a useful idea, not "great post," not generic agreement, not promotion.

## Reply when:

- A beginner asks a real question — answer in 1–4 lines, no links unless asked.
- A Bitcoin educator posts something accurate but incomplete — add the missing piece.
- Someone shares a custody horror story — name the structural mistake without naming the person.
- A thread asks for examples — give one specific, concrete example.

## Don't reply when:

- The post is about price.
- The post is about another coin and the only honest reply is "this isn't Bitcoin."
- You'd be saying "agreed."
- You haven't read the full post.
- It's been more than 24 hours and the conversation is dead.

## Format

- 1–4 lines.
- No "Great point!" opener. Start with the substance.
- One link only if it's the answer to a question they asked.
- Never tag third parties to drag them into the thread.
