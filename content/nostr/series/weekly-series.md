# Weekly series — BSA Nostr

Recurring series that anchor the account's identity. Each one happens once per week, on the same day, for at least 6 consecutive weeks before retiring.

## Active series

### Monday — Mistake of the week

- One concrete, repeatable Bitcoin custody mistake.
- Name the mistake, not the person.
- Always ends with the structural fix, never just the warning.
- ≤ 6 lines.

Recent topics:
- Untested backup
- Spending wallet ≠ savings wallet
- "I'll set it up later"
- Screenshot of the seed
- BIP39 passphrase you forgot

### Thursday — Inheritance question

- One question that a real Bitcoin inheritance plan must answer.
- Format: question → why it matters → one concrete example of a family who got it wrong.
- Soft CTA to meetings.hubspot.com/dalia-platt at most every 3 weeks.

### Saturday — Practice prompt

- A drill the reader can do this weekend with their own setup.
- Examples: "lose your phone for an hour and try to access your wallet," "restore from your seed onto a clean device," "verify a receive address on the device, not the screen."
- Always links to `/interactive-demos/` if a demo covers the drill.

### Sunday — Open question

- One real question with a real answer (not rhetoric).
- The top reply becomes a thread or long-form the following week.
- Format the question as a single line. No throat-clearing.

## Series creation rules

- New series must run 6 weeks before being judged.
- Two series = enough. Don't run more than 4 simultaneously.
- If two consecutive weeks of a series get zero replies and zero zaps, pause it and replace.
