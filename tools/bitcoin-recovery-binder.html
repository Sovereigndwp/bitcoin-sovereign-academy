Print and Assembly Instructions
1
Print single-sided on standard US Letter paper (8.5 × 11 in). Use a laser printer for permanence if possible.
2
Insert tab dividers between each numbered section using a 7-tab binder system.
3
Duplicate Structure Cards (Tab 2) as needed — one card per wallet. Keep blank extras inside the binder.
4
Fill in all fields by hand using permanent black ink. Do not type or digitize this binder's contents.
5
Store in a secure, private location — a fire-resistant safe, safety deposit box, or trusted location known to your designated heir.
6
Review and update annually — log each review in Tab 6. Shred outdated pages and replace them.

Never write seed phrases, private keys, or passphrases inside this binder. This document records locations only.
