Frequently Asked Questions

Is Bitcoin Sovereign Academy free?

Yes! All educational content, interactive demos, and learning paths are completely free. We believe Bitcoin education should be accessible to everyone.

How can I contribute?

We welcome contributions! You can report bugs, suggest features, improve content, or submit code via our GitHub repository. Check out our contributing guidelines to get started.

Do you offer certificates?

We focus on practical skills over credentials. The real certification is your ability to securely self-custody Bitcoin and understand the technology.

I found a bug or security issue. How do I report it?

For general bugs, please open an issue on GitHub. For security-related issues, please contact us directly rather than posting publicly.
