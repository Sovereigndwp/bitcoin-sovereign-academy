⚡ Earn-Back Terms

Last updated: February 2026

The Core Idea: Deposit 50,000 sats to unlock premium content. Complete modules to earn up to 80% back (40,000 sats). Your learning commitment becomes your reward.

1. How It Works

  1. Deposit: Pay 50,000 sats via Lightning to activate Apprentice membership
  2. Learn: Complete interactive modules and quizzes
  3. Earn: Each completed module earns you sats back
  4. Claim: Once earned, sats are tracked for payout (coming Q2 2026)

2. Earning Formula

Sats per module = (Total Deposit × 80%) ÷ Total Modules
= (50,000 × 0.80) ÷ 20 modules
= 2,000 sats per module

3. Module Completion Criteria

A module is considered "complete" when:

  • You have viewed all content in the module
  • You have completed any interactive exercises
  • You have passed the module quiz (if applicable) with ≥70% score
  • Your progress is recorded in your browser's localStorage
✓ What counts:
  • Genuine engagement with content
  • Passing quizzes on your own
  • Completing demos and exercises
✗ What doesn't count:
  • Skipping through content without reading
  • Manipulating localStorage directly
  • Using automated scripts to "complete" modules

4. Example Progress

After completing 10 of 20 modules:
Earned: 10 × 2,000 = 20,000 sats
Remaining to earn: 10 × 2,000 = 20,000 sats
Progress: 50%

5. Payout Process

Current Status: We are tracking earned sats locally. The payout mechanism is launching Q2 2026.

Planned Payout Flow:

  1. Complete modules and track your progress on the dashboard
  2. When you reach your target, request a payout
  3. Provide a Lightning address to receive your earned sats
  4. We verify completion and send payment (1-3 business days)

Minimum Payout:

To request a payout, you must have earned at least 10,000 sats (5 modules completed).

6. Important Limitations

  • Maximum earn-back: 80% of deposit (40,000 sats)
  • Non-refundable deposit: The initial 50,000 sats cannot be refunded; you earn it back through learning
  • Progress is local: If you clear browser data, progress is lost. We cannot recover it.
  • One account per person: Creating multiple accounts to earn more is prohibited
  • Verification: We may verify genuine engagement before payouts

7. What Happens to the 20%?

The remaining 20% (10,000 sats) supports:

  • Platform maintenance and hosting
  • New content development
  • AI Tutor infrastructure
  • Lightning payment processing

See our Transparency Page for how funds are used.

8. Disputes and Edge Cases

If you believe your progress wasn't tracked correctly:

  1. Check your browser's localStorage for bsa-completed-modules
  2. Ensure you're using the same browser/device where you completed modules
  3. Contact us at support@bitcoinsovereign.academy with screenshots

We resolve disputes in good faith but cannot verify activity that wasn't properly tracked.

  • Existing Apprentice members keep their original terms
  • We will announce significant changes via email
