The Reentry Challenge

🔄 High Recidivism Rates

68% of released individuals are rearrested within three years, with financial instability being a primary factor in reoffending and return to incarceration.

💸 Financial Barriers

Released individuals face immediate financial challenges: securing housing, employment, transportation, and basic necessities without understanding financial systems.

🏦 Banking Exclusion

80% of formerly incarcerated individuals are unbanked or underbanked, lacking access to basic financial services needed for economic stability and reintegration.

💰 System Costs

Each returned individual costs taxpayers $80+ per day, while successful reentry programs generate $5-7 in savings for every $1 invested in education and support.
