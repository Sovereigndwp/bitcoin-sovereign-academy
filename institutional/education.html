The Financial Education Gap

📚 Outdated Curriculum

Most financial education materials are decades old, missing digital payments, cryptocurrency, and modern financial technologies that students will encounter.

😴 Low Engagement

Traditional financial education relies on textbooks and lectures, resulting in poor retention and limited practical application of money management skills.

📊 Poor Outcomes

Only 21 states require financial education, and existing programs show minimal impact on student financial behavior and long-term financial wellness.

👩‍🏫 Teacher Challenges

Educators lack proper training and resources to teach financial concepts effectively, often relying on outdated materials and limited real-world experience.
