The Bitcoin Challenge You're Facing

Understanding the specific pressure points helps us design the right educational approach for your organization.

📉 Finance Team Knowledge Gaps

Your CFO and finance team are being asked to evaluate Bitcoin treasury allocation proposals without the foundational education to assess risks, custody requirements, or accounting treatment correctly.

🏛️ Board-Level Unpreparedness

Shareholders and activist investors are asking about Bitcoin strategy. Board members lack the education to engage credibly, evaluate proposals, or lead informed governance discussions.

👨‍💼 Employee Demand for Bitcoin Benefits

Top talent increasingly asks about Bitcoin 401k options, Bitcoin salary allocation, and company Bitcoin policies. Organizations without clear Bitcoin education programs are losing recruiting and retention leverage.

⚠️ Risk Management Blind Spots

Bitcoin custody, counterparty risk, regulatory exposure, and volatility management require specialized knowledge your risk and compliance teams may not have — creating unquantified organizational risk.
