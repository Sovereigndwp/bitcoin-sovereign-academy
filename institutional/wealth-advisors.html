The Bitcoin Challenge You're Facing

Understanding the specific pressure points helps us design the right educational approach for your organization.

🚪 Clients Leaving for Bitcoin-Fluent Advisors

High-net-worth clients who feel their advisor doesn't understand Bitcoin are quietly moving assets to advisors who do. The window to get ahead of this transition is narrowing.

⚖️ Fiduciary Uncertainty Around Bitcoin

RIAs face genuine uncertainty about their fiduciary duty when clients ask about Bitcoin. Without a clear educational foundation, advisors either over-restrict or under-protect — both expose them to liability.

🔐 Custody and Security Knowledge Gaps

Clients ask about self-custody, cold storage, multisig, and inheritance planning for Bitcoin. Most advisors have no framework for these conversations — creating embarrassing knowledge gaps in client meetings.

📋 Tax and Estate Planning Complexity

Bitcoin's unique tax treatment (FIFO/LIFO, hard forks, staking, inheritance step-up) requires specialized knowledge most financial planning software doesn't adequately address yet.
