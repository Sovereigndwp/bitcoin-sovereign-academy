The Bitcoin Challenge You're Facing

Understanding the specific pressure points helps us design the right educational approach for your organization.

📋 Uninformed Policy Decisions

City councils vote on Bitcoin-related mining ordinances, treasury resolutions, and digital payment policies without foundational knowledge — creating regulatory risk and missed economic opportunities.

❓ Constituent Questions Without Answers

Residents increasingly ask city clerks and finance offices about accepting Bitcoin for taxes, permits, and utility payments. Staff lack the training to respond accurately or professionally.

💰 Treasury Diversification Uncertainty

With inflation eroding municipal reserves, Bitcoin presents a credible treasury option. Yet finance officers lack the education to evaluate it objectively alongside legal and fiduciary constraints.

⚡ Missing Economic Development Opportunities

Bitcoin mining operations, node infrastructure, and Bitcoin-native businesses actively relocate to cities with favorable policies. Cities without Bitcoin literacy are invisible to this growing economic wave.
