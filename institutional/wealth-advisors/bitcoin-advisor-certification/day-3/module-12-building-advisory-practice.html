Module 12: Building a Bitcoin Advisory Practice

Goal: Help advisors integrate Bitcoin into their services

Learn how to responsibly introduce Bitcoin advisory services including compliance considerations, client suitability, and risk disclosures.

Compliance Considerations

Regulatory Framework

  • Fiduciary duty: Ensure Bitcoin recommendations align with client's best interest
  • Suitability: Document why Bitcoin is appropriate for each client
  • Risk disclosures: Clearly communicate volatility, custody risks, and regulatory uncertainty
  • Record keeping: Maintain documentation of all Bitcoin-related recommendations

The Bitcoin Advisory Process

Five-Step Framework

  1. Client Education: Ensure understanding before any allocation
  2. Risk Assessment: Use suitability scoring to determine appropriate allocation
  3. Allocation Decision: Start conservative, increase with comfort
  4. Custody Selection: Match custody solution to client profile
  5. Monitoring: Regular reviews, rebalancing, and ongoing education

Discussion: Responsible Bitcoin Advisory

How do you responsibly introduce Bitcoin to traditional portfolios?

  • Start with education, not product
  • Use data-driven allocation frameworks
  • Document everything for compliance
  • Never oversell — let the client's goals drive the conversation
  • Build expertise continuously as the space evolves

Advisor Exercise: Create Your Bitcoin Advisory Process

Time: 45 minutes

Each participant creates a documented Bitcoin advisory process for their practice including:

  1. Client education materials and onboarding steps
  2. Risk assessment criteria and documentation
  3. Allocation framework and decision process
  4. Custody recommendation guidelines
  5. Ongoing monitoring and review schedule

Key Takeaways

  • Bitcoin advisory requires systematic process and documentation
  • Compliance and suitability must be addressed proactively
  • Education-first approach builds trust and reduces liability
  • Advisors who understand Bitcoin deeply will differentiate their practice
