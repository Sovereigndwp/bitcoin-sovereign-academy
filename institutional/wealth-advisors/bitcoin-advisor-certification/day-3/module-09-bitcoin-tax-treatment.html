Module 9: Bitcoin Tax Treatment

Goal: Understand how Bitcoin is taxed

Learn how Bitcoin is classified for tax purposes, how capital gains are calculated, and the reporting requirements advisors must understand.

Bitcoin Tax Classification

Property, Not Currency

The IRS classifies Bitcoin as property. Every disposal — selling, spending, or exchanging — is a taxable event that may trigger capital gains or losses.

  • Buying Bitcoin: Not a taxable event
  • Selling Bitcoin: Capital gains tax applies on the difference between purchase price and sale price
  • Using Bitcoin for purchases: Treated as selling — capital gains apply
  • Receiving Bitcoin as income: Taxed as ordinary income at fair market value

Capital Gains Calculation

Cost Basis Methods

  • FIFO (First In, First Out): Oldest purchases sold first
  • LIFO (Last In, First Out): Newest purchases sold first
  • Specific Identification: Choose which lots to sell

Short-term gains (held less than 1 year) are taxed as ordinary income. Long-term gains (held more than 1 year) receive preferential rates.

Advisor Exercise: Tax Calculation

Time: 30 minutes

Calculate tax outcomes for these sample transactions:

  1. Client bought 1 BTC at $20,000. Sold at $60,000 after 2 years. (Long-term gain: $40,000)
  2. Client bought 0.5 BTC at $50,000. Sold at $30,000 after 6 months. (Short-term loss: $10,000)
  3. Client used 0.1 BTC (cost basis $3,000) to buy a $6,000 item. (Gain: $3,000)

Client Tool: Bitcoin Tax Reporting Checklist

  • Track every purchase with date, amount, and price
  • Record every sale, exchange, or spending event
  • Determine cost basis method with tax advisor
  • Report on Schedule D and Form 8949
  • Consider tax-loss harvesting opportunities
  • Maintain records for minimum 7 years

Key Takeaways

  • Bitcoin is taxed as property — every disposal is a taxable event
  • Cost basis tracking is essential from the first purchase
  • Long-term holding receives preferential tax treatment
  • Advisors should coordinate with tax professionals for complex situations
