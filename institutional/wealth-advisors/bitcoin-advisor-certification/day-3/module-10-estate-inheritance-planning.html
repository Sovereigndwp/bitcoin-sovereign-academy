Module 10: Estate and Inheritance Planning

Goal: Prepare advisors to address long-term planning

Learn the unique risks Bitcoin presents for estate planning and how to design inheritance strategies that ensure family access to digital assets.

Key Inheritance Risks

What Happens Without a Plan?

If a Bitcoin holder dies without a documented access plan, the Bitcoin may be permanently lost. Unlike bank accounts, there is no institution to petition for access.

  • Lost keys: No recovery possible without private keys or seed phrases
  • Lack of documentation: Heirs may not know Bitcoin exists
  • Technical barriers: Family members may lack knowledge to access funds
  • Legal complications: Courts cannot compel the Bitcoin network

Inheritance Solutions

Multisignature for Inheritance

A 2-of-3 multisig where one key is held by a trusted family member ensures continuity. Combined with legal documentation, this provides both security and accessibility.

Trust Integration

Bitcoin can be held within trust structures. The trust documents should include specific instructions for key access and digital asset management.

Advisor Exercise: Inheritance Strategy Design

Time: 45 minutes

Scenario: Client holds $10M in Bitcoin. Design an inheritance strategy considering:

  1. Key recovery procedures for spouse and children
  2. Privacy requirements
  3. Legal documentation needs
  4. Technical access instructions for non-technical heirs

Client Tool: Bitcoin Inheritance Planning Framework

  • Document existence and approximate value of Bitcoin holdings
  • Establish multisig custody with inheritance-friendly key distribution
  • Create sealed instructions for accessing Bitcoin
  • Name a technically competent executor or advisor
  • Review and update plan annually
  • Integrate with existing estate planning documents

Key Takeaways

  • Bitcoin inheritance requires explicit planning — there is no institutional fallback
  • Multisig custody is the most effective tool for inheritance access
  • Legal documentation must include digital asset instructions
  • Regular review ensures the plan stays current
