Complete Curriculum

12 comprehensive modules across three intensive days, plus capstone certification exercise.

Day 1: Bitcoin Foundations for Advisors

Module 1: The Problem Bitcoin Solves

Goal: Understand why Bitcoin exists and why investors care

Explore the history of money, inflation, monetary debasement, and store of value assets. Includes interactive exercise comparing historical returns across asset classes.

Module 2: Bitcoin Monetary Economics

Goal: Understand Bitcoin's monetary design

Fixed supply, halving cycles, mining incentives, and network security. Interactive simulation of supply issuance over time.

Module 3: Bitcoin as a Portfolio Asset

Goal: Understand where Bitcoin fits in portfolios

Risk-return profile, correlation analysis, and volatility vs long-term performance. Historical portfolio simulation exercises.

Module 4: Portfolio Allocation Strategies

Goal: Help advisors decide how to introduce Bitcoin to clients

Position sizing, volatility tolerance, time horizon considerations. Build allocations for different client types.

Day 2: Custody and Security

Module 5: Understanding Bitcoin Custody

Goal: Understand the custody spectrum

Exchange custody, ETF custody, self-custody, collaborative custody. Map solutions across convenience vs security dimensions.

Module 6: Exchange and ETF Custody

Goal: Understand institutional custody options

Custodial exchanges, Bitcoin ETFs, counterparty risk. Analysis of FTX collapse and custodian failure scenarios.

Module 7: Self Custody and Hardware Wallets

Goal: Understand direct ownership

Private keys, hardware wallets, backup strategies. Simulated hardware wallet setup and verification process.

Module 8: Collaborative Custody and Multisignature

Goal: Introduce shared custody solutions

Multisignature wallets, key distribution, recovery planning. Design multisig setup for high-net-worth clients.

Day 3: Tax, Estate Planning, and Client Communication

Module 9: Bitcoin Tax Treatment

Goal: Understand how Bitcoin is taxed

Capital gains, cost basis, tax reporting requirements. Calculate tax outcomes for sample transactions.

Module 10: Estate and Inheritance Planning

Goal: Prepare advisors to address long-term planning

Key inheritance risks, estate planning structures, trust integration. Design inheritance strategy for high-net-worth clients.

Module 11: Client Education and Communication

Goal: Teach advisors how to discuss Bitcoin with clients

Address skepticism, explain volatility, avoid technical overwhelm. Role-play client conversations and objection handling.

Module 12: Building a Bitcoin Advisory Practice

Goal: Help advisors integrate Bitcoin into their services

Compliance considerations, client suitability, risk disclosures. Create comprehensive Bitcoin advisory process.
