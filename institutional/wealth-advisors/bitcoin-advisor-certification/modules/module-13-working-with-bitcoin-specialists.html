Working With Bitcoin Specialists

Goal: Master advisor-specialist collaboration for optimal client outcomes

Learn when to involve Bitcoin specialists, how to evaluate specialist firms, and frameworks for successful collaboration that serves clients while managing advisor liability.

Responsible Bitcoin Guidance Requires Collaboration
Advisors Guide • Specialists Secure

The most effective Bitcoin advisory approach combines advisor expertise in portfolio strategy and client relationships with specialist expertise in technical implementation and security. This collaboration serves clients better while protecting advisors from liability in areas beyond their expertise.

Clear Role Definition

Understanding the distinct responsibilities of advisors vs specialists ensures optimal client service while maintaining appropriate professional boundaries.

Advisor Responsibilities

  • Assess client risk tolerance and suitability
  • Determine appropriate Bitcoin allocation
  • Explain Bitcoin fundamentals and benefits
  • Integrate Bitcoin with overall portfolio strategy
  • Provide ongoing portfolio guidance
  • Coordinate with tax professionals
  • Handle client communication and education
  • Monitor allocation performance

Specialist Responsibilities

  • Design secure custody architecture
  • Implement multisig security setups
  • Manage private key infrastructure
  • Execute secure Bitcoin transactions
  • Provide technical security guidance
  • Design inheritance mechanisms
  • Handle key recovery procedures
  • Maintain operational security protocols

Case Study: High-Net-Worth Client Allocation

Situation: 55-year-old client with $5M portfolio wants 5% Bitcoin allocation ($250K) with self-custody for long-term holding and inheritance planning.

Advisor Role:

  • Confirmed client risk tolerance and investment timeline
  • Validated 5% allocation within portfolio context
  • Explained Bitcoin fundamentals and long-term thesis
  • Coordinated with estate planning attorney

Specialist Role:

  • Designed 2-of-3 multisig custody structure
  • Set up hardware wallets with secure key generation
  • Created inheritance documentation and procedures
  • Provided ongoing security monitoring

Outcome: Client received optimal security without advisor liability, while maintaining advisor relationship for ongoing portfolio management.
