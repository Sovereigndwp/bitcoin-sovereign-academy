Specialist Handoff Memo

The clean handoff is the advisor's best work product. This template keeps your role documented and the specialist fully briefed without a single dangerous detail.

When to send this memo

Real custody, inheritance, recovery, or technical setup work has entered the conversation. Your role is to frame the situation cleanly for the specialist and to document the boundary of what you did. One page, no key material, ever.
