Week 2: How Money Flows

Understanding Income and Expenses

💰 Money is Like Water

Think of money like water flowing through your life. Some flows in (income), some flows out (expenses). The key to financial success is understanding where your money comes from and where it goes!

💵

Income

Money coming IN to you

  • Part-time job
  • Allowance
  • Birthday money
  • Gig work (babysitting, lawn care)
💸

Fixed Expenses

Same amount every month

  • Phone bill
  • Netflix/Spotify
  • School lunch plan
  • Gym membership
🛒

Variable Expenses

Changes month to month

  • Food/snacks
  • Gas money
  • Clothes shopping
  • Entertainment

📝 Before You Start Budgeting

Follow these steps to prepare for successful budgeting:

  1. Gather Your Information
    Look at bank statements, receipts, or just think about your typical week. What money comes in? What do you spend on?
  2. List Your Income Sources
    Part-time job, allowance, side gigs, gifts - write down everything you typically receive in a month.
  3. Track Expenses by Category
    Group your spending: Food, Transportation, Entertainment, Clothes, Phone, Savings, etc.
  4. Use the Budget Tracker
    Add each transaction and watch the visual breakdown show you exactly where your money goes!
💡 Pro Tip for Teens
Start small! Even tracking $20-50 per week helps you understand your money habits. You don't need a huge income to benefit from budgeting.
