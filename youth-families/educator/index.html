🚀 Implementation Timeline

Follow this recommended timeline for successful program launch:

  1. Week 1-2: Planning & Setup
    Schedule facilitator training, review curriculum materials, and prepare venue logistics. Begin student recruitment and family outreach.
  2. Week 3: Facilitator Training
    Attend 90-minute training session covering curriculum overview, facilitation techniques, and technology tools integration.
  3. Week 4: Pre-Program Assessment
    Conduct pre-program assessments with registered students and families. Set baseline measurements for progress tracking.
  4. Week 5-14: Program Delivery
    Deliver 10-week curriculum with weekly sessions. Utilize provided materials, track attendance, and conduct weekly check-ins.
  5. Week 15: Program Completion
    Conduct post-program assessments, distribute certificates, and gather feedback. Plan follow-up activities and continued engagement.
