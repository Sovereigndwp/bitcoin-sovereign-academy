‚úÖ Bitcoin's Solution: Proof-of-Work Voting
Longest chain = majority vote. Dishonest nodes can't outpace honest majority.
The Problem:
Generals must agree on a coordinated attack, but messages can be delayed or corrupted, and some generals might be traitors sending false information.
üîë Bitcoin's Innovation:
Instead of trusting messages, trust computational work. The longest chain represents the most work, therefore the majority vote.
Permissionless Consensus:
Anyone can join without permission. The system works even if you don't know who the participants are or if some are malicious.
