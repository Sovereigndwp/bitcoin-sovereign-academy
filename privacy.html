  • Education should not require surveillance
  • Privacy is a fundamental right, not a feature

4. Local Storage (Your Browser)

Most data stays on your device:

  • bsa-membership: Your membership tier and activation date
  • bsa-completed-modules: Which modules you've finished
  • bsa-earned-sats: Sats earned through Apprentice program
  • bsa-analytics-events: Anonymous usage events (page views, funnel)
  • bsa-assessment: Your learning path quiz results

You can clear this data anytime through your browser settings. We cannot recover it for you.

5. Email Communications

If you subscribe to our newsletter or provide your email:

  • We use it only to send educational content and platform updates
  • We never sell or share your email with third parties
  • You can unsubscribe anytime with one click
  • We use Substack or similar privacy-respecting email providers

6. Payment Processing

Stripe (for Sovereign tier):

  • We do not store your credit card details
  • Stripe processes payments securely under their privacy policy
  • We receive only confirmation of successful payment

Lightning (for Apprentice tier):

  • Lightning payments are pseudonymous by nature
  • We do not link payments to personal identity
  • Payment hashes are stored locally for your records

7. AI Tutor

When you use the AI Tutor:

  • Conversations are processed by the AI provider (Gemini/Anthropic)
  • We do not store your conversations on our servers
  • If using BYOK (Bring Your Own Key), your API key is stored locally in your browser
  • We may use anonymized interaction patterns to improve prompts

8. Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services:

  • GitHub Pages: Hosts our static site
  • Stripe: Processes card payments
  • Alby/Lightning: Processes Lightning payments
  • Substack: Manages newsletter (optional)

Each service has its own privacy policy. We've chosen providers that respect user privacy.

9. Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Session cookies for basic functionality (not tracking)
  • No third-party advertising cookies
  • No cross-site tracking

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (most is in your browser's localStorage)
  • Delete your data (clear localStorage or contact us)
  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time
  • Request information about data we hold

11. Data Retention

  • Local data: Until you clear it
  • Email: Until you unsubscribe
  • Payment records: As required by law (typically 7 years)

12. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us.
