Privacy pre-discovery for advisors

For wealth advisors. A short, copy-pasteable set of questions you can send a Bitcoin-holding client before you sit down to discuss privacy.

This guide is being written

The CoinJoin redesign that links here is shipping ahead of the guides themselves. The page is reserved so the link doesn't 404 and so you can see what's coming.

What this guide will cover (planned outline)

  1. Why pre-discovery matters: walking into a Bitcoin privacy conversation cold is how advisors end up doing client education on the spot, on their own time.
  2. The 8 questions, in plain English, that get the right inputs on the table without asking the client to know technical terms.
  3. How to read the answers: which patterns indicate a custody review, which indicate a privacy review, and which indicate the client is fine.
  4. What you can responsibly say next — and what to refer out.

Outcome: a 10-minute pre-meeting that saves an hour in the meeting itself, framed as client retention rather than custody expertise.
