Personal disclosure audit

A 30-minute exercise. The output is the list of people, companies, and systems that can already match your name to your Bitcoin balance.

What this guide will cover (planned outline)

  1. The four categories of counterparty: regulated (exchanges, brokerages, custodians), commercial (merchants, services, donations), social (friends, family, employees), and adversarial (anyone who scraped a public address you used).
  2. For each category, the worksheet you'll fill in: who, when, which address, what they observed.
  3. Triage rules: which entries warrant rotation, which warrant a CoinJoin pass, which are unfixable and become threat-model inputs.
  4. What to actually do with the result — including when "do nothing" is the right answer.

Outcome: you'll know your real disclosure surface, instead of guessing about it.
