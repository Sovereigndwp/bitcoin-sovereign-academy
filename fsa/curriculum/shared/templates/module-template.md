## 1. Entry Assessment (2-3 minutes)

### Purpose
Adapt content depth based on existing knowledge and confidence levels.

### Questions
**Question 1: Knowledge Check**
- What is [core concept]?
  - A) [Incorrect but plausible]
  - B) [Correct answer]
  - C) [Common misconception]
  - D) [I'm not sure]

**Question 2: Experience Level**
- How comfortable are you with [practical skill]?
  - A) Very comfortable - I do this regularly
  - B) Somewhat comfortable - I understand the basics
  - C) Not comfortable - I need to learn this
  - D) Never heard of it

**Question 3: Confidence Calibration**
- How confident are you that you could [practical application] right now?
  - A) Very confident
  - B) Somewhat confident
  - C) Not very confident
  - D) Not confident at all

### Adaptive Logic
- **High Knowledge/High Confidence:** Skip basic sections, focus on advanced concepts
- **High Knowledge/Low Confidence:** Focus on practice and application
- **Low Knowledge/High Confidence:** Address misconceptions, build foundation
- **Low Knowledge/Low Confidence:** Full module with extra support
