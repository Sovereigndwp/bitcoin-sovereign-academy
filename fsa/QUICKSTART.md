## Key Design Decisions

### 1. **Competency-Based Learning**
Learners advance based on mastery, not time. Adaptive entry assessments adjust content depth.

### 2. **Persona-Optimized Paths**
Five scientifically sequenced paths for:
- Debt-Burdened
- Fresh Graduate  
- Mid-Career
- Pre-Retiree
- Entrepreneur

### 3. **Interactive-First**
Every module includes calculators, real scenarios, and hands-on practice for visceral understanding.

### 4. **7-Element Module Template**
Standardized structure ensures consistency:
1. Entry Assessment
2. Core Content (3-5 sections)
3. Interactive Demo/Calculator
4. Real-World Scenarios
5. Comprehension Check
6. Action Plan
7. Discussion Prompts

### 5. **Organic Bitcoin Discovery**
Bitcoin introduced naturally where principles align (inflation resistance, self-custody, etc.) - not as promotion.
