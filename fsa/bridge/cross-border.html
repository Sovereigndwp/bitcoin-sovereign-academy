What's the Cheapest Way to Send Money?

You know how bank fees work. Now see how they compare to modern alternatives when sending money across borders.

📚 What You Already Know

In Module 3 (Banking Without Getting Robbed), you learned that banks charge fees for almost everything — and international transfers are where those fees are most painful. A $500 wire transfer can cost $25-50 in bank fees alone, plus unfavorable exchange rates.

But banks aren't your only option. Let's compare all the ways to send money internationally.

💡 Key Insight

The fee difference is most dramatic for smaller amounts — exactly the amounts migrant workers send home to their families.

Context: Global remittances total $700+ billion annually. The average fee is 6.2%. That's $43 billion in fees paid primarily by the world's poorest workers.
