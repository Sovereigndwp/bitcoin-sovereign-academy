# Purpose

Use this skill to transform passive content into active teaching material.

# Inputs

Source file or text: $0

# What to do

1. Identify the core concept.
2. Extract the important teaching points.
3. Rebuild the content as a lesson using:
   - learning goal
   - simple explanation
   - why it matters
   - example
   - common mistake
   - reflection question
   - practical next step

4. Remove marketing tone unless explicitly needed.
5. Keep the content structured and learner friendly.

# Output requirements

Return:
- lesson structure
- rewritten lesson content
- notes on what was removed or reorganized

# Quality bar

The result should feel teachable, not just readable.
