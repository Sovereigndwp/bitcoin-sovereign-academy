*Choose one. The selected language becomes the sticky banner at the top of the Loans deep dive (and any other piece touching LMC).*

## Option A — Minimal & transparent (3 lines, ~45 words)

> **Disclosure:** This deep dive is created by Bitcoin Sovereign Academy. Dalia, BSA's founder, advises at The Bitcoin Adviser (TBA), parent of **Loan My Coins**. LMC is one of nine providers covered. All providers evaluated against the same structural framework.

**Tone:** Clinical, fact-first, no rhetoric. Reads like an SEC filing's affiliation note.

---

## Option B — Conversational & honest (4 lines, ~70 words)

> **Heads-up before you read.** BSA's founder Dalia advises at The Bitcoin Adviser, the company behind **Loan My Coins** — one of the nine BTC-backed lenders compared in this piece. We've made a deliberate choice to scrutinize LMC the same way we scrutinize every other product, including pointing out where it isn't the right fit. The math is the math regardless of who built the product.

**Tone:** Direct, human, signals epistemic care without being preachy.

---

## Option C — Formal / regulatory style (4 lines, ~75 words)

> **DISCLOSURE.** Bitcoin Sovereign Academy and its founder maintain an advisory relationship with The Bitcoin Adviser (TBA), parent company of Loan My Coins (LMC). LMC appears in this comparative analysis alongside eight independent competitors. This deep dive is educational in nature and does not constitute financial, legal, or tax advice. Readers are encouraged to verify all rates, terms, and product details directly with each provider before making decisions.

**Tone:** Compliance-grade. Reads like the bottom of a brokerage statement.

---

## Option D — Sovereignty-voice / first-principles (5 lines, ~95 words)

> **Affiliation note.** The Bitcoin Adviser (TBA) owns **Loan My Coins (LMC)**. Dalia, BSA's founder, advises at TBA. LMC is included in this deep dive alongside eight independent BTC-backed lenders, evaluated against the same structural framework — denominator, custody, fee model, hedge feasibility. Part VIII deliberately corrects a common misconception that benefits all lenders (including LMC) to clarify: **BTC-backed loans are not hedges for mortgage liquidation risk**. If this were a marketing piece, that section wouldn't exist. Verify all terms with each provider before deciding.

**Tone:** Voice-spec aligned (BSA's "truthfulness over tone"). Earns trust by openly self-implicating.

---

## My recommendation

**Option D** is the most BSA-voice-aligned and the most differentiated from typical sponsor disclosures. It demonstrates epistemic seriousness rather than just declaring it. The "Part VIII deliberately corrects a misconception" line is a strong signal that this isn't a marketing piece — readers can verify that claim against the actual content.

**Option A** is the safest fallback if you want clinical brevity.

**Avoid Option C** unless legal review demands it — it reads like a brokerage compliance footer and undercuts the editorial voice of the rest of the deep dive.
